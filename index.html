About

Navprayas ( A Group of Innovative Thoughts ) is a voluntary organisation (becomes active particularly during winter and summer holidays) founded in 2000. Our sole aim is to promote social and moral ethics and educational environment among the locals of the so called Engineer's Village Manpur Patwatoli. In Manpur Patwatoli and nearby areas, there was a need of such an organisation which could promote competitive educational environment and women empowerment.Navprayas came forward to deliver these things and is always striving to do more and more for the development and betterment of the society.

Members of the organisation are mostly students studying in colleges(engineering,medical,MBA,commerce etc.) or passouts and some local delegates of Patwatoli. Every year we organise several events like competitive exams(MTSE,Puzzle Race),Freehand Drawing,Rangotsav,Worshops(Vedic Maths,Robotics,Transition),Seminars and Career Counselling. Our main programme which runs throughout the year is Vidya Jyoti - A women literacy programme. This aims at the training of illiterate women in the field of primary education and general awareness.
